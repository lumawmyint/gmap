How to use:

  • Paste your data in the left panel (format: Name TAB Number TAB ... TAB Fifth Column)
  • Click "Process Data" to analyze the input
  • Only entries with fifth column value "1" will be displayed
  • Click on person buttons to view their associated numbers
  • Check/uncheck numbers to mark them with strike-through
  • Double-click on the output area to copy only non-struck-through numbers to clipboard
  • Click the copy icon next to each number to copy individual numbers
  • The system automatically detects different persons and creates buttons for each
